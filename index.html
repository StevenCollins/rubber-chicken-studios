RUBBER CHICKEN STUDIOS

ABOUT RCS

Rubber Chicken Studios is a Canadian indie-games studio based out of Toronto. Founded in 2019 by Lead Designer Steven Collins and Creative Director Michael McKenna to explore new ideas and opportunities in game development. Currently the team is working on publishing "CyberRune: R", a unique Speed-Maze for mobile platforms.

And Now, Lore That Should Go On Another Page

It is the far-flung future of 1997.

As the millennium draws to a close, ever-expanding Hyper-Corps controlled more and more of a dwindling Earth. As resources shrank, and greedy appetites swelled, mankind turned to technology to create its savior. Artificial Intelligence, or "A.I.".

It was a disaster.

Now, mankind lives in the shadow of its greatest accomplishment, brought low by the power and genius of its chosen child. Reduced to surviving in pockets of Free Signal, out of the reaches of the A.I. claws, tribes of humans scratch out a living in the hard earth, with only the barest of Bandwidth.

Powerful Digi-Shamans protect these tiny enclaves, venturing forth into the grim forest of code that is the Hyper-Corp server structure to salvage data, divert power, and most importantly... to hide their people.

These Code-Witches bear with them the only artifact that seems to stem the influence of the mighty A.I.s. The last vestige of a proud Humanity, the last gift of the mighty to their shattered descendants. The Cyber-Runes.

Some give the Cyber-Runner great stealth on the network, others speed. Some provide a measure of control over the great Pathways. But to bring a Cyber-Rune into the network is to arouse the attention of the slumbering Beasts, and the A.I. will be quick to hunt a Cyber-Runner down. The more runes they bear, the more of a threat the A.I. considers them to be, and to bear more than 4 is to risk immediate deletion.

It has been untold years, since the Rise of the Great Minds. The sky is their plaything, and counting time is no longer a surety. But one legend brings the people hope, even now, in these dark days. One tale, of a Cyber-Rune so powerful as to Excise even the A.I.s from the network.

A myth.

A legend.

A false-hope, likely planted by the enemy to lure foolhardy Data-Mages into an early grave.

But if true... It could be the end of an era of tyranny.

That is why, to this day, Cyber-Runners search for the one Rune they have never found.

Cyber-Rune: R.
